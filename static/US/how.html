How It Works

Starting your interior design project with Living Harmony is easy and interactive. As you explore, you can create a profile and begin shaping your design journey at your own pace.

  1. 1. Get Started

    Getting started with Living Harmony is easy. You will create a profile and you can evolve your profile over the course of the project. Your profile is your own personal interior decorating agent. You will be able to develop your ideas with you agent.

  2. 2. Data Gathering

    We collect or sketch your floor plan, take photos, and document important details like layout, lighting, and fixed features.

  3. 3. Site & Environmental Context

    We consider the room’s location in your home, the natural lighting, nearby views, and how the space feels at different times of day.

  4. 4. Functional Layout & Flow

    We define activity zones (seating, working, resting) and ensure easy, intuitive movement throughout the space.

  5. 5. Focal Points & Anchors

    We identify natural visual anchors (like windows or fireplaces) and strategically place major pieces of furniture to support them.

  6. 6. Mood & Style Direction

    We work with you to define a color palette, moodboard, and cohesive design language that reflects your taste and values.

  7. 7. Balance & Visual Composition

    We balance color, light, and material textures to create a harmonious, grounded space with a strong sense of proportion and flow.

  8. 8. Furniture & Decor Selection

    We source or recommend pieces that reflect your goals, lifestyle, and aesthetic—everything from sofas to lighting to art.

  9. 9. Implementation Planning

    We prioritize impactful updates, align them to your budget, and create an easy-to-follow plan for execution.

  10. 10. Review & Refinement

    Once the room is set up, we walk through it with you, tweak layout or decor as needed, and offer tips for ongoing styling or seasonal refreshes.
